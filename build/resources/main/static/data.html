What is a data view?

The data view is designed to explore individual data sets and visualize abundance levels of protein moieties measured by an assay in response to a chemical (small molecule) or genetic (shRNA or CRISPR) perturbation. For targeted proteomics assays, more information can be found in piLINCS.
